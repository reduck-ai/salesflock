# ICP — Former RPA PM

The hard facts — **former** (no longer employed) **senior product manager** at an RPA leader
(UiPath, Automation Anywhere, SS&C Blue Prism) — are checked deterministically in `pre-qualify`
(a LinkedIn company-id match on the experience). By the time this judgment runs, that is already
true. Judge only the **soft** criteria:

- **Customer exposure** — sold to / worked deals with enterprise customers, carried a customer-
  facing or GTM-adjacent remit; not purely internal/technical.
- **US or India based.**
- **Entrepreneurial and active on LinkedIn** — posts, comments, engages; not a dormant profile.
  A founder / builder / advisor signal is a plus.

## Why
- Primary: learn who to sell Reduck to, how, and to whom — these people are the buyers/champions
  and know the enterprise-automation buyer.
- Secondary: one may become our first enterprise-sales hire, so entrepreneurial drive counts.
